FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most asked questions.
What is Cardabase? Cardabase is a mobile app that allows users to easily manage and use their shop club cards.
How much does a cardabase cost? Cardabase is an open-source app, which means no payment is needed. This also means that there are no in-app purchases and hidden fees.
How does Cardabase work? Cardabase uses informations as cards' name and ID, saves thm and emulates them.
Can I import my existing cards into Cardabase? Yes, Cardabase provides an option to import cards by using your phone's camera or manually entering EAN-13 numbers. Soon you will be able to export all the cards in one file and import it to a new device.
Is Cardabase available for both iOS and Android? Not yet, Cardabase is only available to Android users via sideloading.
Is my data safe and secure with Cardabase? Absolutely! Cardabase takes data security seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely on your device (offline). No internet needed. No servers. No online world. Strong encryption.
